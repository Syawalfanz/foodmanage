About FoodManage

We built FoodManage to help local Malaysian F&B businesses replace scattered spreadsheets and handwritten records with a clean, affordable, and fully web-based management system.

Our Mission

FoodManage was developed as part of the BIW20303 Web Design course at Universiti Tun Hussein Onn Malaysia (UTHM). Our goal was to design a real-world solution for a genuine problem: small and medium F&B businesses in Malaysia that cannot afford expensive POS systems but still need organised, digital tools to run their operations effectively.

The platform centralises menu management, inventory tracking, and business analytics in one responsive, zero-cost web interface — making professional-grade management tools accessible to every local restaurant, cafe, and food stall in Malaysia.

Our Core Values

The principles that guided every design and development decision.

Accessibility

Every feature is designed for users with low to moderate technical proficiency. No training required.

Efficiency

Every key action — adding an item, updating stock, checking reports — is reachable in two clicks or fewer.
